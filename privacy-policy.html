Privacy Policy for Social Media Campaign Tool

Last Updated: December 18, 2024

Overview

This application helps social media managers create content campaigns by analyzing public social media posts.

Information We Collect

  • User Account Information: Email, name (via Google OAuth)
  • Public Social Media Content: URLs of public posts that users provide
  • Generated Content: Campaign posts created by our AI

How We Use Information

  • To provide the campaign generation service
  • To improve our AI models
  • To authenticate users

Data We Access from Facebook

When you provide a Facebook post URL:

  • We read public post content (text, images, videos)
  • We do NOT access private posts or personal data
  • We only process content you explicitly provide

Data Storage

  • User credentials are stored securely
  • Generated campaigns are stored in your account
  • Public post content is temporarily cached for processing

Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth for authentication
  • Supabase for database
  • OpenAI/Gemini for AI generation

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with third parties except:

  • Service providers necessary for app functionality
  • When required by law

Your Rights

  • Access your data anytime
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your generated campaigns
