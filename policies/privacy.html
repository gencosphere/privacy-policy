Privacy Policy - TikTok Integration

Effective Date: December 24, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how [Your App Name] ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you connect your TikTok account to our social media management platform.

2. Information We Collect from TikTok

When you authorize our app to connect with your TikTok account, we collect:

  • Profile Information: Username, display name, profile picture URL, and TikTok user ID
  • Access Tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens to authenticate API requests
  • Video Metadata: Information about videos you upload through our platform (title, caption, hashtags)

3. How We Use TikTok Data

We use the information collected from TikTok to:

  • Display your TikTok account information in our dashboard
  • Upload videos to your TikTok account as drafts via the Content Posting API
  • Schedule and manage your TikTok content publishing
  • Provide analytics and insights about your scheduled posts

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All TikTok access tokens are encrypted at rest in our database
  • Secure Transmission: All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to encrypted data
  • Data Retention: We retain TikTok tokens only while your account is connected. Upon disconnection, all tokens are immediately deleted.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your TikTok data to third parties
  • Share your TikTok information with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes beyond scheduling and publishing content

We MAY share data with:

  • TikTok: When uploading videos via their API (required for functionality)
  • Service Providers: Secure cloud hosting providers (e.g., AWS, Supabase) who are contractually obligated to protect your data

6. Your Rights and Control

You have the right to:

  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your TikTok account anytime from Settings → Connected Accounts
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of all your data by contacting us (see below)
  • TikTok Authorization Management: Revoke our app's permissions directly in TikTok → Settings → Privacy → Manage Apps

7. TikTok-Specific Information

  • Scopes Used: user.info.basic, video.upload, video.publish
  • API Usage: We use TikTok's Content Posting API to upload videos as drafts. You maintain full control and must complete publishing within the TikTok app.
  • No Automatic Posting: We never publish videos without your explicit review and approval in TikTok.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of our TikTok integration after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at:

  • Email: support@nutrifixai.com
  • Website: nutrifixai.com

10. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and applicable data protection laws including GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California).
